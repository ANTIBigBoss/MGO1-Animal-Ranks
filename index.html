Requirements for MGO1 Rankings

F.H (FOXHOUND)

Requirements
Req. 1: Achieve the top rank on any leaderboard in Metal Gear Online, and maintain that position to retain the rank of Foxhound.

Req. 2: The ratio of (total kills + total stuns against enemy) to (total deaths + total stuns) must be 1.5 or above.

Req. 3: The ratio of (total amount of sneaking mission rounds you stayed alive and total amount of rescue rounds you stayed alive) to (total rounds) must be 0.65 or above.

Req. 4: The ratio of (the number of total team victories - number of total sneaking victories) to (the number of total capture rounds + total rescue rounds + total team deathmatch rounds) must be 0.65 or above.

As a member of FOXHOUND, you are a highly skilled operative, capable of achieving and maintaining top positions on leaderboards and contributing significantly to team victories. You have a high kill/stun ratio and are skilled at staying alive in sneaking mission and rescue rounds. Your formula for success includes taking out enemies while minimizing deaths and supporting your team to achieve victory.

Formula 1: (total kills + total stuns against enemy) / (total deaths + total stuns) ≥ 1.5

Formula 2: (total amount of sneaking mission rounds you stayed alive and total amount of rescue rounds you stayed alive) / total rounds ≥ 0.65

Formula 3: (the number of total team victories - number of total sneaking victories) / (the number of total capture rounds + total rescue rounds + total team deathmatch rounds) ≥ 0.65

TAR (TARANTULA)

Requirements

This means that you are an effective team player, able to contribute significantly to your team's success in battle.

Formula: (Total team kills + total team stuns) / (total amount of kills + total amount of stuns against enemy) 0.05

PGE (PIGEON)

Requirements
Req. 1: The amount of kills in capture divided by the amount of stuns you have in capture = 0.5 or below.

Req. 2: The amount of kills in capture divided by the amount of stuns you have in capture = 0.5 or below.

This means that you rely more on stunning your opponents rather than killing them in the capture game mode.


Formula 1: (Amount of kills in capture) / (Amount of stuns in capture) 0.5

Formula 2: (Amount of kills in rescue) / (Amount of stuns in rescue) 0.5

F.S (FLYING SQUIRREL)

Requirements
Req. 1: (Total number of rolls) divided by (amount of total rounds played) = 10 or above.

This means that you are an agile and evasive fighter, able to move quickly and unpredictably during battle.

Formula: (Total number of rolls) / (amount of total rounds played) 10

S.E (SNAKE EATER)

Requirements
Req. 1: (Total playtime of sneaking mission) divided by (total playtime) = 0.75 or above.


This means that you are an expert in stealth, able to complete sneaking missions with ease and efficiency.

Formula: (Total playtime of sneaking mission) / (total playtime) 0.75

KER (KEROTAN)

Requirements
Req. 1: (Total playtime of Capture Mission) divided by (total playtime) = 0.75 or above.

This means that you are skilled at completing capture missions, dedicating a significant portion of your playtime to mastering this game mode.

Formula: (Total playtime of Capture Mission) / (total playtime) 0.75

SLO (SLOTH)

Requirements
Req. 1: The amount of headshots received you have divided by the total amount of deaths received you have received must be 0.35 or more.

This means that you are a tough fighter who can take a lot of damage and stay in the fight.


Formula: (Amount of headshots received) / (Total amount of deaths received) 0.35

N.O (NIGHT OWL)

Requirements
Req. 1: Your total amount of thermal goggle use divided by your total playtime must be 0.05 or above.

This means that you are a master of night vision, using thermal goggles to see in the dark and gain an advantage over your enemies.

Formula: (Total amount of thermal goggle use) / (Total playtime) 0.05

PIG (PIG)

Requirements
Req. 1: Your total amount of food eaten divided by the total amount of rounds played must be 3 or above.

This means that you are able to keep your hunger satisfied and stay focused during long battles, giving you a strategic advantage over your enemies.

Formula: (Total amount of food eaten) / (Total amount of rounds played) 3
